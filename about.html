About Chalet Tuftra

Where every detail tells a story of Alpine elegance and authentic hospitality

Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Zermatt's Findelbach district, Chalet Tuftra stands as a testament to the perfect marriage of traditional Alpine architecture and contemporary luxury. Our philosophy centers on three fundamental elements that define the ultimate mountain retreat: space, view, and light.

Every room in our chalet has been thoughtfully designed to maximize these elements, creating an atmosphere where families can gather, memories are made, and the stresses of everyday life simply melt away against the backdrop of the majestic Matterhorn.
