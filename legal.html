Legal Information

Terms, conditions, and policies governing your stay at Chalet Tuftra

Terms and Conditions

Booking and Reservations

All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation. A deposit of 50% is required to secure your reservation, with the balance due 30 days prior to arrival. Bookings made within 30 days of arrival require full payment at the time of booking.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made more than 60 days prior to arrival will receive a full refund minus a 5% administrative fee. Cancellations made 30-60 days prior to arrival will forfeit 50% of the total booking value. Cancellations made less than 30 days prior to arrival will forfeit the full booking amount.

House Rules

  • Check-in: 4:00 PM | Check-out: 11:00 AM
  • No smoking inside the chalet
  • Pets welcome with prior arrangement
  • Quiet hours: 10:00 PM - 8:00 AM
  • Maximum occupancy as per booking agreement

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to provide our accommodation services, including name, contact details, payment information, and preferences to enhance your stay experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for booking management, service delivery, and communication regarding your stay. We do not share personal information with third parties except as required for booking processing and legal compliance.
